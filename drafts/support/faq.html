Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions below.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unworn items. Simply contact our support team with your order number and we'll provide a prepaid return label. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for 2-3 business day delivery. Free shipping is available on orders over $75.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination. Customs duties and taxes may apply.

How do I find my shoe size?

We provide a detailed size guide on each product page. You can also use our virtual fitting tool that measures your foot using your phone's camera. If you're between sizes, we recommend going half a size up.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Please contact support immediately if you need to make changes.
